Hi, my name is Cheyenne!

I grew up in the san fernando Valley. For as long as I can remember, I've been obsessed with all things style. About a year ago, I expanded my horizons, and decided to give Cosmetology a try, and I've loved it! I've always been good with hair and cosmetics, but now attending school has increased my capabilities ten-fold! Since attending my cosmetology school, I've mainly specialized in doing hair, which I have only gotten better at over time. Keep up with this page to stay updated on my hairdressing adventures, and book an appointment with me anytime you would like. As I learn more, I also invite more challenge for me to learn and grow as a cosmetologist, so if there are any styles you would like to experiment with, please come and have me try them out, we can both learn about our preferences together!!!
